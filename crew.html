About the Production

A+ Studios

A+ Studios is an independent production house operating out of Alingsås, Sweden. JONATHAN: The Tricycle Heist is the studio's debut short. The film is being produced on a budget of approximately the change found between the studio sofa cushions, plus several bags of M&Ms.

The Pitch

A 12-minute crime short. Tight locations. Practical effects. Heavy on the dark comedy, heavier on the property damage. Targeting submission to short-film festivals and online release. Jonathan II will follow if Jonathan I doesn't get us arrested.
