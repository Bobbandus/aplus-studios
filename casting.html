The Sequel · In Pre-Production

JONATHAN II

// JAILBREAK ON WHEELS

Three months into a twenty-five year sentence, Jonathan Reeves receives a letter. Inside the envelope: a photograph of his dog. On the back, in handwriting he doesn't recognise, three words: "Bring the cash."

Within forty-eight hours he will be the most wanted man in the country. Within seventy-two, he will be doing eighty miles an hour the wrong way down a freeway on three wheels.

Bigger. Louder. Dumber. The dog is back. The tricycle is back. The cartel is, regrettably, also back.

A+ Studios is now looking for the following positions for JONATHAN II: Jailbreak on Wheels. No experience required. Some experience preferred. M&Ms accepted as portfolio.
