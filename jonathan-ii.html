COMING · MMXXVII
Logline — Working Draft

A man, a tricycle,
and twenty-five
to life.

Three months into a twenty-five year sentence, Jonathan Reeves receives a letter. Inside the envelope: a photograph of his dog. On the back, in handwriting he doesn't recognise, three words.

Within forty-eight hours he will be the most wanted man in the country. Within seventy-two, he will be doing eighty miles an hour the wrong way down a freeway on three wheels. JONATHAN II is bigger, louder, dumber. The dog is back. The tricycle is back. The cartel is, regrettably, also back.
